Frequntly Asked Questions

What is so special about WigMaid?

All our wigs are made by hand, made to your specific measurements and with the most ethical materials available. Our wig linings are breathable, high quality French or Swiss lace. Safe for bald heads.

What is your refund policy?

If for any reason, you are dissatisfied or have simply changed your mind, we have a no qualms refund policy, full refund will be given within 90 days of the order.

Are your wigs safe for alopecia clients?

Yes 100%. Our clientele comprises to a large extent of clients with alopecia. Our wigs are comfortable to wear all day, every day, or as required.

How do I wash my wig?

Wash wig as required, gently with lukewarm water and a mild shampoo. Allow to air dry.

Do you make wihs for afro hair

Yes, we absolutely make wigs for all hair types. You will have the option to specify what hair type you need on the order page.

Why are your wigs more expensive?

Natural ingredients cost more than syntheticmaterials. Are wigs are also 100% handmade, we only use high quality materials to ensure your ultimate comfort and wig longevity. We are confident that you will love the quality.

How long will my wig last for?

With proper care, your wig unit will last for up to 12 months with everyday wear. We are confident that you will love the quality.
